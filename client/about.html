About

The Site-Verifier project was inspired by our team's collective passion for eSite Verifier and the need for a reliable tool to verify the integrity of email addresses. It started as a Portfolio Project at ALX software engineering, where we were tasked with creating a practical application that solves a real-world problem.

Throughout the development process, we collaborated closely as a team, leveraging our diverse skills and knowledge to create a robust and user-friendly email validation tool. The project has been a great learning experience, allowing us to apply the technologies and principles we learned during our studies.
